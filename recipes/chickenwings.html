Crispy Chicken Wings

EXTRA CRISPY CHICKEN WINGS THAT WILL BE A HIT AT YOUR NEXT PARTY!

It took me some time to create the recipe I am sharing today. If you want a chicken wing that will give you that initial fried chicken crunch, with a moist and meaty afterbite, then read on below.

These wings are a hit at every party, BBQ, or game day I go to and are always the number one requested dish that I make. The key lies in one major factor, twice fried.

Ingredients

  • 16 Full Chicken Wings (we will be cutting each peice in half)
  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1/4 Cup of Baking Powder
  • 2tbsp Paprika
  • 2tbsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 2tbsp Chili Powder
  • 3tbsp Black Pepper
  • 4tbsp Salt
  • 2tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 2 Bottles of Buffalo Wing Sauce
  • 1/2 cup of BBQ sauce
  • 3tbsp Honey
  • 1.5qt of Canola Oil
    •

    Step by Step

    1. Pour the Canola Oil into your Deep Fryer and warm to 375 degrees
    2. Cut all your chicken wings so there is one drumstick and one flat per wing
    3. In a bowl mix your Flour, Baking Powder, Paprika, Cayenne Pepper, Chili Powder, Black Pepper, Salt, and Garlic Powder into a bowl. Mix the ingredients well.
    4. In a seperate bowl mix your Buffalo Wing Sauce, BBQ sauce, and Honey and whisk
    5. Dust in each chicken wing in the flour and place in basket
    6. Once the basket is full, lower into preheated oil.
    7. Cook each wing for approximately 10 minutes or until the skin is a dark golden brown.
    8. Transport chicken wings on cooling rack. Repeat until all chicken wings are cooked.
    9. Once all wings are fried repeat the process a second time.
    10. Once all chicken wings are cooked, roll wings in your mixed wing sauce.
    11. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce!
